Events in Zhanaozen: gas price will be reduced

At the moment, the owners of gas stations in the Mangystau region, within the framework of the social responsibility of business, have proactively made a decision to reduce the cost of gas from 120 to 85-90 tenge per liter.

As a reminder, on January 2, residents of Zhanaozen took to the streets demanding to lower prices for liquefied gas. Later, actions were also recorded on the streets of Aktau. The protesters blocked the roads in Aktau and Zhanaozen. The President of Kazakhstan, Kassym-Jomart Tokayev, reacted to the situation and instructed the government to urgently consider the situation in Zhanaozen, taking into account the economic feasibility in the legal field.

What will the weather be like in Kazakhstan on January 4-6

"In the north-west, north, center of the country, the thermometer will drop to 20-25 at night, sometimes 30 frost," forecasters predict.

"From January 4, a series of diving cyclones with large reserves of heat and moisture will affect the weather in most of the republic, causing precipitation on its way, mainly snow, increased wind with a snowstorm, and an increase in air temperature," Kazhydromet said.
